About

Script Terror is a blog about agile software engineering by Norman Steger.

Norman is a full stack software engineer with a passion for JavaScript and TypeScript based technology stacks living in Regensburg, Germany. He is most passionate about:

  • user experience
  • agile methodology
  • test driven development
  • behavior driven development

Currently he works as a consultant at Comsysto Reply GmbH, where he helps customers in industries like automotive, telecommunications, insurance, transportation or finance to build digital experiences for tomorrow.
