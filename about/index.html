About

Script Terror is a blog about web software development by Norman Steger.

Norman is a Software Engineer with a preference for JavaScript and TypeScript based technology stacks located in Regensburg, Germany. While also not shy of working on webservices in the backend, he is most enthusiastic about:

  • user experience
  • agile methodology
  • test driven development
  • behavior driven development

Currently he works as a consultant at Comsysto Reply GmbH, where he helps customers in industries like automotive, telecommunications, insurance, transportation or finance to build digital experiences for tomorrow.
